What is Unity-PONG?

Unity-PONG is a recreation of the classic 2D sport game PONG made with Unity.

You can check how I made the game in the following video.

How to play?

The objective is to take the maximum amount of points by hitting a ball with a paddle and bring it on the other side of the playfield.
Each time the ball goes behind the other player paddle it will score one point to the player.

There are two modes available in this game.

Player vs Player
Two players compete for win the match.

Player vs AI
One player compete against an AI. The AI can be set to three types of difficulty.

It is required a mouse and a keyboard for play the game.
